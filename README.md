# 📘 StudyBuddy – Your Study Compass

> Turning scattered study plans into a clear, structured path of progress.

---

## 🎯 Problem

Many students struggle with:
- Unclear study plans  
- Poor time management  
- No strategy for revision  
- Feeling overwhelmed before exams  

This leads to stress, inconsistency, and inefficient learning.

---

## 💡 Solution

**StudyBuddy** is a logic-based academic planner that helps students:

- Organize their study schedule  
- Allocate time based on subject difficulty  
- Track progress effectively  

It transforms **unstructured study → structured execution**.

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. User inputs:
   - Total number of chapters
   - Available days
   - Difficulty level (Easy / Medium / Hard)
   - Daily study hours  

2. The system:
   - Distributes chapters intelligently  
   - Allocates time based on difficulty  
   - Generates a daily study plan  

3. Output:
   - Clear and structured study schedule  
   - Helps maintain consistency and focus
